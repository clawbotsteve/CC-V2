The Psychology: Free taste → low-friction trial → monthly habit → upgrade for power

---

TIER 0: FREE (forever)
1 image generation. That's it. No credits, no timer. One single photo to see the magic.
Watermarked output
Forces signup (captures email for marketing)
Goal: "Holy shit that's good" moment → immediately wants more

TIER 1: STARTER TRIAL — $0 for 24 hours
50 credits for 24 hours (enough for ~40 images, 2 short videos, or 1 avatar training)
Full tool access — no feature gates
No watermark
Requires payment method on file (like Netflix free trial)
Auto-converts to Starter plan after 24h unless cancelled
Goal: Let them actually USE the platform, get hooked, have content they don't want to lose

WHY THIS STRUCTURE:

$19.99 entry instead of $29.95 — breaks the $20 psychological barrier. Way more signups.
24hr trial with payment method — conversion rates are 60-80% vs 2-5% for "free tier with no card." People forget to cancel, and by then they're hooked.
3 tiers not 4 (plus free + trial) — less decision paralysis. Starter/Creator/Studio is dead simple.
Simpler credit costs — round numbers (1, 2, 3, 5, 10, 20, etc.) instead of 11, 22, 51, 101. Users can do math in their head.
Credit packs as escape valve — when someone runs out mid-month, they buy a pack instead of upgrading. Pure profit.
Studio at $149.99 with API access — captures the agency market without a custom enterprise tier.
Same margins — still 120%+ on all tools, just cleaner presentation.
