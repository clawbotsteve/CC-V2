8) MONTHLY VS ANNUAL EFFECTIVE OUTPUT COUNTS
==================================================
Current pricing UI applies annual discount = 20% off price.
Assuming credits/month stay the same by tier:

- Monthly outputs: same as matrix above.
- Annual outputs over 12 months: monthly outputs x 12 (for each tool).
- Effective output-per-dollar gain with 20% annual discount:
  -> approximately +25% output per $ vs monthly pricing.

Example (Starter, kling_audio_5s):
- Monthly: 20 clips/month
- Annual total: 240 clips/year
- Per-dollar efficiency: ~25% better than monthly billing

9) GROSS MARGIN ESTIMATE PER MODEL ENDPOINT (FRAMEWORK)
==================================================
Exact margin requires provider COGS per generation (internal billing data).
Use this formula per tool variant:

- Revenue per generation = (plan monthly price / plan monthly credits) * tool credit cost
- Gross Margin % = (Revenue per generation - Provider Cost per generation) / Revenue per generation
